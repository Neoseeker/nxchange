nxchange has been shut down and is no longer available.

nxchange was launched by Neoseeker in 2004 as a project to allow users to buy, sell, or trade used video games to each other. The project was a work of love, but never gained traction. Since 2010 the site has been inactive and only remained online for posterity. In 2014, Neoseeker shut the site down permanently, removing all pages. This 404 page has been left here to leave a bit of history behind on the great design and great dreams of the site.
